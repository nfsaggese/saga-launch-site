Our Goals in Action

We believe it’s important to balance commitments to consumers, businesses, and the world.

For Consumers

  • To be aware of their full scope of options.
  • To understand each of their options individually and where they sit in a comparative context.
  • To always pay less than or equal to the amount of value they recieve.

Action Plan

  • Our product models experiences such that people can quickly access and learn about all of their options.
  • Our product contains details and relevant information about fishing experiences that is useful when making decisions.

For Businesses

  • To empower businesses who offer great products and services to have consistent financial success.
  • To serve entrepreneurs by creating free tools that act as a partner in growing their ventures from day one.
  • To give small businesses the infrastructure to compete with economies of scale.

Action Plan

  • Our product for fishing guides will help great guides become financially successful guides.
  • Our product is affordable for entrepreneurs because it's free!
  • As our product gains economies of scale, we will be able to negotiate collectively on behalf of small businesses to give them advantages often only privileged to large businesses, such as lower credit card processing rates.

For the World

  • To help consumers identify businesses that maintain high standards of social responsiblity and environmental sustainability.
  • To provide an outlet for consumers & businesses to contribute to envrionmental, social, and philanthropic initiatives.
  • To help businesses and consumers adopt socially responsible and environmentally sustainable behavior.

Action Plan

  • Our product will provide businesses who meet environmental sustainability and social responsibility standards with badges and an explanation of how they recieved it.
  • Our product will include business analytics tools that provide insight into consumer preferences regarding sustianability and social responsiblity so that business owners can recognize that sustianable and socially responsible practices are not only good for the world, but also for their business.
