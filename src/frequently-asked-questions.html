FAQs

How much will Saga cost at launch?
Our software is free for our guides and always will be. The price you set is the price you will receive after credit card processing fees.
How does Saga make money?
We charge 5% to the customer as a booking fee. This goes to help support the continued development of our software, servers, messaging, routing and scheduling calls, and our staff of starving college students.

How much are credit card processing fees?
The current rate is 2.9% + 30 cents per transaction. This is a very low rate already, but we hope to further decrease it over time as more guides start using Saga.
Why does Saga pay credit card processing too?
We split the credit card processing fees with you in order to demonstrate that we are giving you these rates at cost and not marking them up in any way. This way we are actually incentivized ourselves to continually negotiate with credit card processors to lower rates on your behalf.
What is the setup process for Saga?
Once you provide us your login details, our team will backup your site and complete the installation for free. If you want to complete the installation yourself or use your own "web guy," we can assist you in doing so for free.
What if I already a have a booking feature?
For Saga to work properly, we will have to remove any booking forms or plugins that your site currently has and replace them with our software.
Who made the icons found on this site?
We used icons made by graphic designers found on thenounproject.com. Special thanks to Juraj Sedlák and Ron Scott.
